About Me:


I am a very competitive and goal oriented person, always looking for a challenge. After spending about 5 years in the healthcare field as a claims auditor, I wasn't feeling challenged enough in my career, and was becoming increasingly bored. I always had a love for fitness, and I developed a passion for helping other people reach their personal fitness goals, so I joined the 9Round franchise and started up my own fitness studio called 9Round Kickboxing.

Since October of 2016, I have built a successful fitness business that is now managed and run by a great team of employess, and I decided to use my free time to develop new skillsets. I was introduced to the world of web development through Eleven Fifty Academy, and the challenge of bringing someone's vision alive through a web page has been very exciting, giving a great sense of accomplishment. I look forward to a long career in web development, and using these skills as another avenue for helping clients/businesses reach their goals!
